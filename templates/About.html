Through the use of a responsive web app, we aim to create a network of locally run kitchens for the food insecure.
After a year of worsening conditions of the most vulnerable in your community, we now provide a way for YOU to give a helping hand!
      MealQuest provides weekly volunteering opportunities in supported communities, and enables you to donate to a communal fund.

How does MealQuest work?

Who can get involved?

Students

- Help out those in need in your community
- Get an opportunity to have weekly experiences in a communal kitchen
- Make new friends and have fun
- Gain volunteering and hospitality work experience 

Mentors

- Use MealQuest resources to organise events
- Mentor students and protect their well-being and health
- Help improve your community and have some fun doing it

Businesses

- Make your business more sustainable
- Gain a new way of reaching eco-conscious customers
- Increase your goodwill
- Help develop a new generation

Community

- Make your business more sustainable
- Gain a new way of reaching eco-conscious customers
- Increase your goodwill
- Help develop a new generation

How does MealQuest keep running?

With your help!

Encourage businesses in your area to become a MealQuest host, tell your friends to join as volunteers, and help bring in more mentors for your community!

MealQuest's aim is to help out your community. That's why we run on donations only.  No ads, complete privacy for its users, and no paid downloads.
